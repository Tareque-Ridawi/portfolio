Tick-Cross JavaScript (v1.0)



Welcome to Tick-Cross JavaScript. Enjoy playing this game on your browser without wasting paper. Make less litter, Save the Environment.

How to play :This is a offline multiplayer game. Tap any box to start the game. The first one to tap will get the tick (✔️). After the first player taps it will be the second player's turn. The second player will get cross (✖️). After the second player again it will be first player's turn and the game will go on like this till anyone can match three boxes vertically, horizentally, top left to bottom right or top right to bottom left. The one who can match like this first wins the game.
